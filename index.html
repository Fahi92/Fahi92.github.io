Bouncing Balls

Designed and animated multiple bouncing balls with unique patterns and realistic motion physics using Maya. Through careful manipulation of time sliders and precise keyframing, I created dynamic movements that accurately simulated the physics of bouncing balls.

ABOUT

Hi there! My name is Fahima Begum. I am a passionate animator dedicated to bringing characters and stories to life. With a deep love for storytelling and a keen eye for details, I strive to create visuals that engage and captivate audiences. Animation has always allowed me to express my creativity and imagination. I believe that a well-crafted animation can evoke emotions, transport viewers to new worlds, and leave a lasting impact.
